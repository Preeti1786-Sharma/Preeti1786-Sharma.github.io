End-of-Life (EOL) & NOK Process Transformation – Philips Medical Systems

PROBLEM

Fragmented Non-Quality (NOK) handling and inconsistent End-of-Life (EOL) decision processes were leading to operational inefficiencies and reduced supply chain visibility across global teams.

APPROACH

Designed and standardized a structured NOK management framework integrated with supply chain workflows, improving classification, escalation, and cross-functional alignment.

Simultaneously contributed to improving EOL decision clarity by introducing structured lifecycle visibility inputs for better operational governance.

OUTCOME
96% reduction in operational disruptions
Improved process consistency & governance adoption

IMPACT NOTE

Process adoption and operational impact were acknowledged through internal leadership communication during implementation rollout.

Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard – Power BI Decision Support System

PROBLEM

Lack of consolidated visibility into key performance indicators (KPIs) such as revenue trends, profitability, and category performance.

SOLUTION

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to consolidate business metrics and enable structured decision-making through visual analytics.

OUTCOME
Improved KPI visibility & reporting efficiency
Enabled data-driven decision-making insights

SKILLS USED

Power BI · Data Modeling · KPI Design · Business Intelligence
