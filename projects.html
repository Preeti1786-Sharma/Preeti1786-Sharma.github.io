NOK Check Process Design – Supply Chain Compliance (IGT-S)

Executive Summary

Designed a structured NOK Check process during internship at Philips to improve early detection of regulatory and quality blocks in global supply chain operations.

Situation

NOK checks were previously performed only at order release and delivery stages, leading to reactive issue handling and operational delays across global manufacturing sites.

Action

Built a structured NOK Check framework by collaborating with Supply Chain, Quality, and Regulatory stakeholders across Best, Pune, and Suzhou to introduce early-stage validation.

Shifted checks to three stages: pre-release (3 weeks before), release, and delivery, enabling proactive identification of blocked materials.

Impact
96% reduction in waiting time
93% reduction in operational stops

Improved shipment reliability, reduced supply chain disruptions, and strengthened compliance by preventing blocked material flow.

Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard – Power BI Decision Support System

Executive Summary

Developed a Power BI dashboard to improve visibility of supply chain and business KPIs for faster decision-making.

Problem

KPI data was fragmented across multiple sources, limiting real-time visibility and delaying decision-making.

Solution

Designed an interactive Power BI dashboard consolidating revenue, profitability, and operational metrics into a single reporting layer.

Impact
Improved KPI visibility
Faster decision-making cycles

Enabled data-driven insights for business and supply chain performance tracking.
