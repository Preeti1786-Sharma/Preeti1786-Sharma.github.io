End-of-Life (EOL) & NOK Process Transformation – Philips Medical Systems

PROBLEM

Fragmented Non-Quality (NOK) handling and inconsistent End-of-Life (EOL) decision processes were leading to operational inefficiencies and reduced supply chain visibility across global teams.

APPROACH

1. Lifecycle Visibility Assessment
Mapped product portfolios to identify inconsistent or delayed EOL classification.

2. Standardization of Decision Inputs
Aligned key parameters influencing EOL decisions: demand trends, inventory position, serviceability requirements, and regulatory constraints.

3. Cross-Functional Alignment
Introduced structured coordination between planning, supply chain, and operations teams to reduce fragmented decision-making.

4. Decision Acceleration Logic
Improved clarity of inputs required for EOL approval discussions, reducing iterative delays.

OUTCOME
96% reduction in operational disruptions
Improved governance consistency

Strengthened lifecycle governance and improved alignment across global supply chain stakeholders through structured EOL decision-making.

IMPACT NOTE

Achieved measurable operational improvement following implementation, as highlighted in internal leadership communications during rollout.

Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard – Power BI Decision Support System

PROBLEM

Lack of consolidated visibility into key performance indicators across business and supply chain functions, resulting in fragmented reporting and delayed decision-making.

APPROACH

1. Data Consolidation
Identified and structured relevant datasets for KPI tracking across business functions.

2. Dashboard Development
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to visualize revenue trends, profitability, and category-level performance.

3. KPI Structuring
Defined standardized KPIs to ensure consistent reporting and interpretation across stakeholders.

4. Insight Enablement
Enabled structured decision-making through visual analytics and performance tracking.

OUTCOME
Improved KPI visibility
Faster decision-making support

Strengthened data-driven decision-making by enabling consolidated visibility of business performance metrics.

SKILLS

Power BI · Data Modeling · KPI Design · Business Intelligence · Supply Chain Analytics
